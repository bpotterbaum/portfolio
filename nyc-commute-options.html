Commute scenarios

1. Stay in Tennessee, fly in

  • Best airport: BNA, with nonstop service into NYC airports.
  • Probably the best bridge if this really is a temporary 2027-era solve.
  • Financially plausible, emotionally expensive.

2. East Coast family move, then train / ferry in

  • Best long-ish-term quality-of-life setup if you must preserve the job.
  • Targets calm suburbs, waterfront towns, and strong school districts.
  • Much less brutal than weekly flights if this goes longer than expected.

3. Drive-only areas

  • There are options, but they tend to be the least elegant for Midtown.
  • Mostly useful if you want more space and can tolerate a rougher daily rhythm.
